Discover Grindelwald

Surrounded by glaciers, waterfalls and lush valleys, Grindelwald is a dream village for hikers, skiers and scenery lovers. Wander through wooden chalets, ride gondolas to skywalk platforms and enjoy sunsets over the Eiger from your private balcony.
